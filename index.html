The Project

As part of the UMSI 699: UX Mastery Course, we have developed a prototype for Panorama Detroit. This project sprung out of a desire to reconnect people with public spaces, specifically public art, during the pandemic. It is a way to be part of your community, while choosing the interaction level that best suits your needs.   • Listen in or discuss with local artists and historians about the past, present, and future of mural, monuments, and moments in your community
  • Plan and Post walking tours to help individuals in your community better engage the spaces around them
